A Christmas Carol

The woman sitting across from Connor Reid had broken the silence of an otherwise client-free evening. Beneath her perfect sculpture of dark brown hair, the woman’s green eyes sparked with an equal measure of anger and fear. Connor was used to a healthy dose of anger directed his way, but fear, that was something new. He watched her slim, well-manicured fingers raise a cigarette to her lips.
